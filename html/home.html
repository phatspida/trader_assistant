Day Trading

Day trading is a trading strategy that involves buying and selling financial instruments within the same trading day.

Traders aim to capitalize on short-term price movements, often using technical analysis and chart patterns to make quick decisions.

This approach requires a keen understanding of market trends, risk management, and the ability to react swiftly to market changes.
